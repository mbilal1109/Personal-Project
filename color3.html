Third favorite color

The color brown is a warm color that stimulates the appetite. While it is sometimes considered dull, it also represents steadfastness, simplicity, friendliness, dependability, and health. It's often seen as solid, much like the earth, and it is a color often associated with resilience, security, and safety. Brown can also create feelings of loneliness, sadness, and isolation. Brown is symbolic of the earth and was often the color of a monk's robe, signifying humility and God's connection with commonplace and the ordinary. Brown was the second color choice for both men and women for their living rooms and the fourth choice for their bedrooms.
