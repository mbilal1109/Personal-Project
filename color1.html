First favorite color

Blue is a cool color that stands for spirituality, intelligence, and creativity. It is a relaxing color to look at and often prevents chaos. You will see the blue color is used often at hospitals, and airlines as it symbolizes trust, loyalty and strength. It is considered a spiritual color in some cultures and traditions. For example, the Virgin Mary is generally depicted wearing blue robes. Blue is also associated with the Jewish faith because it is used for Hanukkah decorations. Most of the time blue is considered a masculine color, so many male toddlers wear blueish clothing and play with blue toys. Blue can make a huge impact when combined with warm colors like red and yellow.
